Our Story

Why this community exists

Let's Farm It Out was created by a group of long time Farming Simulator buddies who believes multiplayer should be more than simply joining a server and harvesting fields. Great communities are built on trust, consistency and people who genuinely enjoy helping one another.

After spending years in online gaming communities, one thing became clear. Players stay because of the people, not the equipment or the maps. A well run community creates an environment where everyone knows what is expected, disagreements are handled fairly and players can simply enjoy the game.

This community was built to combine organized leadership with a relaxed atmosphere. We believe structure should support the experience, not control it. Rules should be clear, consistently enforced and designed to help everyone have a better time, never to take the fun away.

Whether you're planting your very first field or you're an experienced virtual farmer looking for a long term home, our goal is simple: create a place where people look forward to logging in, working together and coming back tomorrow.

FAQ

Common questions before joining

What platforms are supported?

We welcome players across all platforms, XBox, Playstation and PC.

Do I need prior multiplayer experience?

No, but it helps. Having a basic understanding for Farming Simulator will take you a long way and make multiplayer servers much more enjoyable.

How do I join?

First, Let's Farm It Out! Find us in the multiplayer list of servers under LetsFarmItOut. Then if you like us, join us on discord from the links above. We are sure you will want to connect again.
